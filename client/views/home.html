Welcome to RNE Furniture

The Rustic Furniture Company featuring the work of Derek Skapars

At RNE Furniture, we have coffee tables, dining tables, half-moon tables, end tables, night stands, walking sticks and much more. We also have a long list of custom orders we've made upon request, which you can view in our product gallery. Each piece is designed by Derek Skapars himself and carries the company's signature spiral, giving each piece a character not found in any other furniture.

We here at Rustic New England Furniture take enormous pride in creating our products. Custom orders are always welcome and constantly in production and in-stock items are always being sold or replicated, so we would be happy to show you or make you something you really like! We even accept PayPal to make owning a piece of our funiture as easy as possible.
